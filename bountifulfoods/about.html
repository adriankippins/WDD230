About Us

Bountiful Foods is a leading provider of organic, locally grown foods in the coastal areas of Southern California. We are dedicated to delivering fresh and healthy food options to the local population and tourists, supporting healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience.

Our History

At Bountiful Foods, we have a rich history that spans over three decades. It all started with our founder, Roberto Kippins, who had a vision of bringing fresh, locally sourced produce to the community. In 1990, he opened our first storefront in Carlsbad, California, offering a wide variety of fruits and vegetables directly from the farmers in the area.

Over the years, our commitment to quality and sustainability allowed us to expand our operations. We established partnerships with local farmers and suppliers, ensuring that our customers receive the freshest and highest quality products available. In addition to our storefront, we introduced food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego, making it even more convenient for people to access our products.

Today, Bountiful Foods is a trusted name in the industry, known for our dedication to providing fresh and healthy food options. We continue to innovate and adapt to the changing needs of our customers, while staying true to our core values of promoting local agriculture and supporting the community.
