Overview

Site name:

Bountiful Foods

Purpose:

The Bountiful Foods website serves as an online platform for selling fruit juices and juice mixes. The website's primary purpose is to provide a user-friendly and convenient way for customers to explore and purchase a variety of fresh and healthy fruit-based products. The website aims to promote the mission of Bountiful Foods, which is to offer organic, locally grown fruits and produce to both the local population and tourists visiting the coastal areas.

Website Logo

The site logo & favicon, exclusively designed and created by Roberto Kippins, adds a unique touch to this site project. The logo colors will be used ot set the color theme for the entire site.
