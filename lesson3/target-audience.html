Target Audience

Description

The target audience for the Georgetown Chamber of Commerce website primarily includes local businesses, entrepreneurs, and professionals in Georgetown, Guyana. This includes small and medium-sized businesses across various industries, startups, individual entrepreneurs, and aspiring business professionals. Additionally, the website may also be of interest to investors, government agencies, and organizations supporting local business development. The target audience seeks networking opportunities, resources, and support to grow their businesses, collaborate, and contribute to the economic growth of Georgetown.
