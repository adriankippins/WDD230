Target Audience

Description

The target audience for the Bountiful Foods website primarily consists of individuals residing in Carlsbad, California, and the surrounding areas. The website aims to cater to specific groups within the local population listed in the scenario section.

Scenarios

  1. Health-Conscious Individuals: This includes individuals who prioritize fresh and organic food in their diets. They actively seek out nutritious options and value the benefits of consuming wholesome, locally grown produce.
  2. Fitness Enthusiasts: The website will target fitness enthusiasts who engage in regular physical activities and are conscious of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. They seek out nutritious food options to support their fitness goals and overall well-being.
  3. Parents: Bountiful Foods recognizes the importance of providing healthy options for children. Parents looking for fresh and natural fruit juices for their kids will be a key target audience. The website will emphasize the nutritional value and benefits of the products for children's growth and development.
  4. Local Community: The website aims to engage with the local community in Carlsbad and nearby areas. This includes individuals interested in supporting local businesses, promoting sustainability, and enjoying the convenience of accessing fresh and organic food options within their community.
